New Horizons News Feed

Streams news and posts from Bluesky, and threads from Hacker News.

This website runs entirely in your browser using the Bluesky and Hacker News API, with no data sent anywhere. No analytics or trackers are used, so you can explore freely. All JavaScript was developed with the help of LLMs like Google AI, Deep Seek and Claude. If you want to browse the Bluesky way, here are the links. You can pin it to your home.

As more companies, institutions, and organizations join Bluesky, you can use it as a one stop solution for all kinds of updates. All post data comes from BlueSky, where the accounts on the list have posted it. This project simply aggregates and visualizes that data in a news media format. It's part of my ongoing experience with the BlueSky API.

"New Horizons" refers to exploring or discovering new areas, possibilities, or experiences that were previously unknown or uncharted. The term often symbolizes growth, expansion, and the opportunity to move beyond current limitations, seeking fresh opportunities or insights. Inspired by the New Horizons satellite.
You can pin the list in the Bluesky app for direct access to news, free from algorithms. If you choose the feed, it follows HN Ranking from the past 12 hours.
